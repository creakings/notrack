Security
What Username should I use?

You can have any choice of username, it doesn’t have to be related to your system logon name.
Try to avoid easily guessed usernames like Admin, Administrator, System, or Root.

What can I do if I forget the password?

You can remove the password by manually editing the /etc/notrack/notrack.conf Delete the lines starting “Username” and “Password”

sudo nano /var/www/html/admin/settings/front.php

What is auto-throttling?

NoTrack contains an auto-throttling feature to prevent attacks from certain IP addresses or to specific usernames.

Can I use with Fail2ban?

Yes, NoTrack writes a log of failed password attempts into /var/log/ntrk-admin.log

Can I have more than one account?

No, the complexity dramatically increases to add multiple accounts, and at this point it is not a feature I'm prepared to add.
